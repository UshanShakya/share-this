Logo meaning

The knot is a single continuous path connecting two colored dots — one purple, one amber. The dots represent two people. The path between them is the shared canvas. The name "Knoodle" merges knot (connection) with noodle (to sketch, doodle). The double-o in the wordmark mirrors the loop shape and is always tinted pink.

Usage rules

Don'tRecolor the knot path to anything other than pink
Don'tRemove the two endpoint dots
